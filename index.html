About Russell

The East Texas Trial Attorney Who Shows Up

Facing a legal battle in Texas is overwhelming. The stakes are high, the process is complex, and choosing the wrong attorney can cost you everything — not just money, but time, reputation, and peace of mind.

Most people don't know where to turn when they're dealing with a medical malpractice claim, a premises liability dispute, a contract gone wrong, or a wrongful employment termination. The courtroom is not a place for inexperience.

Three Decades of Proven Results

Russell R. Smith has spent over three decades in State and Federal Courts across East Texas, handling the full spectrum of civil litigation — from oil and gas disputes to nursing home negligence, from insurance litigation to toxic tort cases.

His Magna Cum Laude J.D. from South Texas College of Law, combined with a Finance degree (Summa Cum Laude) from UT Austin, gives Russell a rare combination of legal precision and analytical firepower. He doesn't just argue cases — he understands the financial, business, and human stakes behind every one of them.

Trusted by clients across Nacogdoches, East Texas, and beyond. As the current President of the Texas Association of Defense Counsel (2025–2026), Russell stands at the forefront of legal advocacy in Texas.
